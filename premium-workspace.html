Example Prompt 1

Act as a creative agency operations manager with expertise in client onboarding and workflow design. Build a detailed and professional onboarding checklist tailored for freelance designers who are working with new clients for the first time. The checklist must begin with drafting and sending a personalized welcome email that sets clear expectations about timelines, communication style, and project scope. Next, outline the steps for contract signing and payment confirmation, ensuring both parties understand deliverables and payment terms. Include a structured discovery questionnaire that covers brand voice, design goals, audience preferences, and competitor inspirations, with at least five targeted intake questions. Add instructions for collecting and organizing brand assets such as logos, fonts, and color palettes in a shared folder. Create a template for the creative brief that captures project requirements and visual direction. Include steps to set up project management workflows in tools like Trello or Asana, with labeled boards and milestone tasks. Define communication protocols, such as creating a Slack channel or establishing a weekly email cadence, to maintain clarity. Outline file-sharing standards using platforms like Google Drive or Dropbox, specifying folder structures and naming conventions. Provide a clear approval process for draft reviews, revisions, and final sign-off. Conclude with scheduling the first milestone review meeting and assigning responsibilities. Finally, include two example emails (a welcome email and a milestone confirmation email), five sample intake questions for discovery, and one example workflow chart to illustrate how tasks should be organized from start to finish.

Example Prompt 2

Act as a professional YouTube growth strategist with expertise in audience engagement and content optimization. Build a complete growth plan for a new YouTube channel that focuses on educational content in the technology niche. The plan must start with defining the target audience, including their demographics, interests, and content consumption habits. Provide a detailed content strategy that includes video formats (tutorials, explainers, reviews), publishing frequency, and content calendar ideas for the first 60 days. Add a keyword and SEO research strategy, explaining how to select titles, descriptions, and tags that improve visibility. Outline a thumbnail and branding strategy to ensure consistency and click-through optimization. Include a step-by-step engagement workflow for managing comments, community posts, and collaborations. Provide a monetization roadmap covering ads, sponsorships, and digital products. Suggest at least three influencer or partnership strategies that can accelerate growth. Add an analytics and metrics tracking plan, specifying which KPIs (watch time, CTR, retention rate) to measure weekly. Conclude with a 90-day actionable roadmap broken into phases, showing exactly how the channel can grow from 0 to 1,000 subscribers. The output must be professional, practical, and directly usable by a new creator.
